pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made of flatbread topped with ingredients like tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked at high heat.

origin

Modern pizza evolved from flatbread dishes in Naples, Italy.

cooking

Traditionally baked quickly at a very high temperature.

Toppings

Can include a wide variety of ingredients like meats (pepperoni, sausage), vegetables, herbs, and spices.
